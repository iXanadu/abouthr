Cities
Top
What is it?

Cities are the core geographic regions of Hampton Roads. Each city has its own detail page on the public site with restaurants, attractions, events, and local information.

How it appears on the site

Cities appear in the main navigation dropdown, on the homepage as clickable cards, and each has a dedicated page showing all venues and local information.

Venues
Top
What is it?

Venues are places of interest within each city: restaurants, cafes/breweries, attractions, events, and beaches. They help relocating families discover what each city has to offer.

How it appears on the site

Venues appear on their city's detail page, organized by type in tabs. Featured venues may appear in highlight sections.

Venue Types
  • Restaurant - Dining establishments (use Cuisine Type to categorize)
  • Cafe/Brewery - Coffee shops, breweries, casual spots
  • Attraction - Museums, parks, entertainment venues
  • Event - Annual festivals, recurring events
  • Beach - Public beach access points

Image Guidelines
Top
General Guidelines
  • Format: JPEG for photos, PNG for graphics with transparency
  • Quality: High resolution but web-optimized (under 500KB ideal)
  • Rights: Only use images you have rights to (owned, licensed, or royalty-free)
Recommended Dimensions
Image TypeDimensionsAspect Ratio
City banners1920 x 600 px16:5 (wide landscape)
Venue photos800 x 600 px4:3 (standard landscape)
Team headshots400 x 400 px1:1 (square)
Testimonial photos200 x 200 px1:1 (square)
Destination cards800 x 500 px8:5 (landscape)
Content Tips
  • Cities: Iconic landmarks, skylines, or lifestyle scenes
  • Venues: Exterior shots, signature dishes, or atmosphere photos
  • Military: Base gates, aerial views (check public affairs guidelines)
  • Team: Professional headshots with consistent backgrounds
