About Hampton Roads

Military Hub

Home to the largest naval base in the world. 25% of the nation's active-duty military personnel reside here.

Rich Culture

Vibrant arts scenes, diverse culinary experiences, and multicultural traditions from Colonial Williamsburg to Virginia Beach.

Natural Beauty

Miles of Atlantic beaches, the Chesapeake Bay, scenic rivers, and lush state parks for endless outdoor recreation.
