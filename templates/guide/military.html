Military Relocation

Hampton Roads is proudly home to some of the most significant military bases in the U.S.

Hampton Roads is proudly home to some of the most significant and strategically important military bases in the U.S., including Langley Air Force Base and Naval Station Norfolk, the largest naval base in the world.

25% of the nation's active-duty military personnel reside here, and they are often celebrated and honored through various community events and tributes, promoting the strong connection between the civilian population and those both currently and formerly enlisted in our nation's military.
